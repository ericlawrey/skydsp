Welcome to Sky DSP

Here at Sky DSP we specialise in developing wireless systems, including OFDM systems and software radio. This website provides an information source on OFDM technology, which is an emerging key method for obtaining high speed wireless access.

Currently a range of publications are provided on OFDM, however in the near future a range of OFDM simulation tools will be available.
