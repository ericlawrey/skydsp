Welcome to Sky DSP

This site hosts on my undergraduate thesis (1997), PhD (2001) and associated research papers (2001) on wireless communications.

The site was called skydsp because I was looking for a short domain name and I originally had plans to start a business specialising in Digital Signal Processing (DSP). In the long run I ended up on a different path and now am focused on developing knowledge systems that help capture and communicate environmental knowledge of Australia's marine environment. This site looks old, because it is. It has largely been unchanged since 2002. I maintain this site to ensure any references to these publications still work.
