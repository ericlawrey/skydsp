The performance of a single antenna base station was simulated. The results here show the signal to interference ratio of the WLAN building and surrounding areas. The scale is in dB. The two numbers on the plots show the performance as the interferer moves, which is shown as a white cross. The top number shows the average spectral efficiency that could be achieved if the system were using adaptive modulation, while the lower number shows the percentage area of the building which has a signal to interference ratio below 10 dB. This represents the quality of service, where the lower the number the better.
