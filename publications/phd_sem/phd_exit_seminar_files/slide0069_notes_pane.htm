The above plot shows the allocated modulation scheme. The simulated system subdivided the frequency into 160 subcarriers and updated the modulation scheme every 1 cm of movement. Also added was a delay of 1 cm in the modulation allocation. This was to simulate the time taken to perform the SNR estimation and reallocation of the modulation scheme. The modulation varies from none (Blue areas) through to 6 bits per Hz per sec (64 QAM).
