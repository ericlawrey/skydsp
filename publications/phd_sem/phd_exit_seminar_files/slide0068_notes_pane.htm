One of the Frequency Selective Fading measurements was used to simulate the performance of an adaptive modulation system. The above plot shows the resulting SNR as a function frequency and distance moved for a fixed transmission power of –8 dBm. The average SNR is about 17 dB, however there are many nulls in the spectrum where the SNR is below 5 dB. This SNR was chosen because the spectral efficiency of the resulting adaptive modulation was 2 bits/Hz/sec allowing it to be directly compared with the performance of QPSK.
