There are currently several OFDM system already in operation. Digital Audio Broadcasting is the longest running system, having started operation in 1996. It transmits CD quality compress digital audio, replacing the FM broadcast system. It can transmit 6-10 CD quality channels over a 1.5 MHz system bandwidth.
Digital Video Broadcasting started in operation in Australia in Jan 2001, and will replace the current analog system by around 2008. It transmits MPEG-2 compress video at data rates up to 31 Mbps. This is sufficient to support High Definition Television.
