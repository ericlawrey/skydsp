An experiment was performed to investigate the how frequency selective fading varies with small changes in space. The results of this investigation was used to measure the performance of the proposed adaptive OFDM system, which allocates the subcarrier modulation scheme based on the signal to noise ratio of each subcarrier.
The experiment was performed by measuring the power frequency response of an indoor radio channel using a sweeping signal generator and a spectrum analyser. The receiver antenna was then moved along a 2.4 m guide track in steps of 1 cm. The frequency response of the channel was measured for each movement of the antenna.
